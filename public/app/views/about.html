About

Hemingway is like a cat shelter. We aim to find a new home for our wonderfully used books. Though none of these books are curated, we know for a fact that we have some damn good books. Browse around and we hope you find what you want.

Technologies Used:

  • Frameworks et al: MongoDB, ExpressJS, AngularJS and Node (aka the MEAN stack), Foundation v5 for its wonderful grid, a ton of CSS, Gulp.js and bower
  • Components: Angular-Foundation, Angular-Payments (with Stripe), FontAwesome, Hover.css, Animate.css
